# BareMediator Usage Licensing and Pricing

BareMediator is licensed under MIT license and free for individuals and companies with less than USD 500,000 in ARR. Above that threshold, check [licensing and pricing](PRICING.md).

## Pricing

Above 500,000 USD/ARR, the pricing is:

- USD 24/year every USD 500,000 ARR
- Pro-rated to the cent between thresholds
- ARR to be considered is the one at the end of the previous fiscal year

### Formula
Round up((ARR in USD) * 24 / 500,000) = Annual Fee in USD

#### Examples
- 500k ARR → $24/year
- 1M ARR → $48/year
- 1.5M ARR → $60/year
- 2M ARR → $96/year
